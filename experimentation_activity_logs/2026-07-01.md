### 🔍 Key Observations
1. **Successful Transfer of Q4 Optimizations:** The prompt evolved on Q4 for the `financial` task transferred exceptionally well to FP16, boosting performance from 60.85% (baseline/FP16 prompt) to **67.88%**. On `svamp`, the Q4 evolved prompt transferred to FP16 also showed improvement (73.33% vs. 70.00% baseline).
2. **Transfer Resistance / Specialization:** Some prompts evolved on Q4/FP16 did not transfer well. For example, on `gsm8k`, transferring prompts between precisions resulted in lower performance than the baseline.
3. **Structured Extraction Resilience:** In the `financial` task, the evolved Q4 prompt improved performance on both Q4 (55.81% ➔ 64.94%) and FP16 (60.85% ➔ 67.88%).
